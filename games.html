A Variety of Fun

You may be amazed at how many games and toys you can buy for 100 yen. Magicians can expand their trick repertoire with these gimmicks. There's even a set of dice. 6 dice in a pack and in 3 different sizes. Who knows what you need different sized dice for, but they've got them if you need them. Perhaps you can play Bomberman in real life with those inflatable bombs? You've also got echo mics and tambourines for when you're pretending to be a rockstar at home. There are even puzzle games like the "Move the Ball -Three Way-", which looks to be fairly difficult.
